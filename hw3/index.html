Multiplier Generator

This is an multiplier generator. Enter the numbers that you want to multiply. The first input is the multiplier. The second input is the multiplicand. The third and fourth input is the length of the the maximum number of multiplier and multiplicand. The starting number should be less than the ending number. The starting number cannot be less than -50 and the ending number cannot be greater than 50.
